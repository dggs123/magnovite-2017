The Campus Ambassador program undertaken by Magnovite Fest, Christ University primarily aims to incorporate participation from students spread across the nation in various colleges.

A Campus Ambassador is an official representative of Magnovite Fest in his/her college and is the essential link between the two. As a Campus Ambassador you shall be coordinating with Magnovite Fest to ensure maximum participation at Christ University from your college. This can be achieved through:
  • Word of Mouth
  • Posters
  • Online publicity of Magnovite Fest through social media in your city and especially in your college
  • Helping out Magnovite Fest to reach out to your college in any manner you deem fit.

This is a good opportunity for students looking to undertake a position of responsibility and gain an exposure to the much needed communication and managerial skills. Campus Ambassadors are an important part of the extended family of Magnovite Fest which is acknowledged with a certificate from the Christ University, along with other benefits as enlisted.
